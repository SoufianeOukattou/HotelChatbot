A detox break by the pool
During the day, Le Bar de la Piscine offers a deliciously
healthy selection of juices made with fruit and vegetables
straight from the hotel’s kitchen garden. Savour creative
mocktails such as the «Secret Garden» that combines
cucumber, garden mint, lemon juice and green apple,
«Tropical Beet» that is an audacious mix of beetroot,
banana, mango and apple juice, or «Avocalada» in which
avocado delicately lifts a delicious melange of pineapple
juice, coconut cream and orgeat syrup... Everything you
need to stimulate the senses and boost your va-va-voom!
To get the evening off to a good start, the menu also includes
exclusive house cocktails - one of La Mamounia’s finest
specialities - as well as a deliciously light, white house sangria.
A selection of starters, subtly seasoned with spices,
coriander
