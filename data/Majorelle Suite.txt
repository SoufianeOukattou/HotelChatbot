Majorelle Suite
Surface area  114 sqm
Up to 4 people
King-size bed and twin beds
Balconies
Garden and Pool View


Overlooking the flower-filled Gardens, swimming
pool and Atlas Mountains, the Majorelle Suite
spans 114 m² and comprises two bedrooms. The
first features a double bed; the second two singles
facing a rotunda-style window whose views
beckon you to gaze out over the hotel Gardens.
The suite also boasts two bathrooms with separate
shower and bathtub.
In homage to the artist, Majorelle blue
punctuates the décor of the suite, enlivened
by touches of orange and enhanced by
shimmering zellige mosaics.
