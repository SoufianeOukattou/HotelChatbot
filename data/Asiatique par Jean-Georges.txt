Intimate and refined
L’Asiatique par Jean-Georges restaurant is one of
finest creations to have emerged from La Mamounia’s
latest renovation.
It is a place of many influences, both in terms of its
stunning setting and its menu, which will take you on
a journey through a multitude of Far Eastern flavours.
From the original restaurant, the Patrick Jouin - Sanjit
Manku duo have chosen to retain the superb traditional
surroundings, revamping them with furnishings that
play on the restaurant’s graceful lines and contrasting
pale wood with deep indigo blue fabrics.
The light fittings create a warm and intimate
atmosphere.
And both inside and out, the establishment is as
exotic as a map of the world, reimagined by talented
explorers of design and décor.
