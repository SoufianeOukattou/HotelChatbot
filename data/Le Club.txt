A true Moroccan lounge
Perched on the top floor of the Le Marocain restaurant,
Le Club is one of La Mamounia’s best kept secrets.
Escape here to admire the setting sun over a pre- or postdinner cocktail, linger under the tent before heading out onto
the bar terrace with its beautiful views over the hotel Gardens,
and soak up an enchanting mixture of music and calm...
Here, cocktails and tapas dishes make up a menu with a
distinctly Moroccan feel.
The cocktail menu invites you to sample a selection
of argan oil, orange blossom, honey, amlou (a Berber
preparation made from honey and almonds) and dates in
its “Creations from the Garden” mix.
While the tapas menu features delights such as carrot
hummus roasted in cumin-flavoured olive oil and
served with batbout bread, crispy aubergine enhanced
with roasted almonds and honey, and sardine roulades
seasoned with coriander, to name but a few. 

Opening times
The bar is open every day from 05 :00pm to 01 :00am
