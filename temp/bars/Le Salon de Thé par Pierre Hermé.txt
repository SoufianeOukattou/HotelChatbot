A gourmet break in the heart
of La Mamounia
A magical place born of the hotel’s 2020 metamorphosis,
Le Salon de Thé par Pierre Hermé is one of the many
delightful features of La Mamounia.
Calm and welcoming, it occupies an intimate space,
complete with inviting sofas and armchairs, which
opens out into the radiant light of its Andalusian patio.
The atmosphere here is that of a winter garden, with
the gentle babbling of the marble fountain that seems
to rise up from the ground to greet the tea room’s
monumental, crystal chandelier.
This enchanting setting plays on contrasts of sleek
modernity, with the beautiful deep blue seats by Patrick
Jouin, juxtaposed against the delicate ornamental
motifs of the traditional Moroccan floor lamps.
