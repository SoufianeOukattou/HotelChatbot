In honour of Sir Winston
Named in honour of Sir Winston, who considered La
Mamounia to be one of the most beautiful places in the
world - spending his winters here with his beloved wife,
Clementine - Le Churchill Bar is a Marrakech institution.
Today, it has been transformed into a luxurious Pullman
carriage in honour of the hotel’s railway past. It offers a
haven of smoky oak with sumptuously back-lit walls, in
which the materials and lighting combine to create an
intimate and stylish space.
In its centre, a superb, black marble counter, interspersed
with translucent blocks of vibrant green lights by designer
Patrick Jouin, welcomes guests to the bar.
The result is a timeless atmosphere, beautifully
incorporating contemporary touches, in which to
savour the finest of champagnes and spirits. 

Opening times
The bar is open every day from 05 :30pm to 01 :00am
