Magnificent Riad deep in the hotel Gardens
Welcome to this magnificent Riad deep in the hotel
Gardens, with its three floors showcasing the splendours
of Arabo-Andalusian architecture. An exceptional setting
in which to savour Moroccan specialities prepared with
true passion by Chef Rachid Agouray.
If Le Marocain has earned its place as an institution
in Marrakech for over thirty years, and this is no
coincidence; it’s a result of the exquisite synergy
between the venue and its culinary offerings.
As soon as you arrive, you will be transported into the
splendours of a magnificent palace, where alcoves
welcome you like tiny jewel cases lined with soft
cushions.
Your dining experience will be a true voyage of
initiation, beginning with the ritual of ftour - a harira
soup served with dates, chbakias, honey and sesame
pastries - a combination as surprising as it is delicious.
