Rome in the heart of Marrakech
Thanks to the magic of a new décor created by designer duo
Patrick Jouin and Sangit Manku, you will find yourself in a light-filled
Roman trattoria. A winter garden decorated with delightful frescoes
of playful cats, furnishings in subtle almond green tones, and large
picture windows opening onto the surrounding vegetation.
The pleasure of an aperitif, the spectacle of the cuisine being
prepared before your very eyes on the central island, the
irresistible aroma of pizzas cooking in the large wood-fired
oven... The hardest part?
Deciding which dishes will transport you to the very essence
of la dolce vita!
It is a lively, friendly place, animated by the choreography of
the kitchen team working on the central island, and matched
by the smiling and attentive staff.
A venue bathed in light and fresh air that is in close proximity
with nature, offering a seamless transition between the dining
room and garden thanks to its covered terrace.
